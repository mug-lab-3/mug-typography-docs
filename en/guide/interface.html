Interface Overview ​

Selection Mode ​

Selection Mode switches between the three levels described in “Core Concepts”: Global, Character, and Part. With 3D presets, you can also select Camera mode to control the camera. Clicks and drags in the viewer act on the mode selected here.

ParameterTarget LevelDescriptionDefault Shortcut
GlobalGlobalSelects the entire textWinAlt+Ctrl+A/MacOption+Command+A
CharacterCharacterSelects individual charactersWinAlt+Ctrl+C/MacOption+Command+C
PartPartSelects path elements within charactersWinAlt+Ctrl+P/MacOption+Command+PUnavailable in Lite presets
CameraCameraDisplays dedicated on-screen controls for operating the cameraShown only for 3D presets

Switch modes using Selection Mode at the top of the Inspector.

NOTE

Characters and parts excluded from rendering in Output Settings cannot be selected by clicking or marquee selection.

Viewer Controls ​

In the viewer, you can drag targets directly or use modifier keys and on-screen controls to transform and select them.

Moving ​

Click and drag a character or part to move it.

  • Clicking an already selected target preserves the multiple selection and moves all selected targets together.
  • Clicking a different target switches the selection to it.

Displaying Rotation, Scale, and Pivot Handles ​

Hold WinCtrl/MacCommand and click an already selected target to display the rotation, scale, and pivot handles.

HandlePositionAction
Rotation handleCircle extending from the pivotDrag left or right to rotate
Scale handleRectangle extending from the pivotDrag toward or away from the pivot to scale
Pivot handleCross at the center, where the rotation and scale handles beginDrag to move the reference point for rotation and scaling

NOTE

The handles appear only when you WinCtrl/MacCommand + click an already selected target. They do not appear for an unselected target.

Stretch ​

Hold WinAlt/MacOption and drag an edge of the bounding box—top, bottom, left, or right to change Stretch in that direction. This affects the selected targets.

Adjusting Tracking and Margins ​

Press WinAlt+↑/↓/MacOption+↑/↓ to increase or decrease the value in 0.1 em increments.

ModeTarget
GlobalTracking (global character spacing)
CharacterMargin After for the selected characters
PartUnavailable

While WinAlt/MacOption is held, the preview shows the Tracking and Margin After range for each character. The value currently being adjusted—Tracking in Global mode or Margin After in Character mode—is highlighted with Previewing Color.

Marquee Selection ​

Drag through an empty area containing no characters or parts to display a selection rectangle and select all targets inside it.

While dragging, elements inside the selection rectangle are highlighted in real time with Selected Color; elements outside it are shown with Previewing Color.

Camera Mode (3D Presets) ​

Camera is added to Selection Mode for Mug Typography 3D and Mug Typography Lite (3D). Selecting it displays dedicated controls in the viewer, allowing you to adjust the composition without moving back and forth to the Inspector.

ActionResult
Drag the Orbit handleChanges camera Yaw/Pitch
WinAlt/MacOption + drag an empty areaOrbits the camera
Drag the Target PadMoves the camera target
Drag the Zoom handle left or rightChanges Camera Zoom
Drag the Perspective handle left or rightChanges the strength of perspective
Drag an empty area away from the controlsChanges Lens Shift
Hold Shift while draggingLocks movement to the axis with the greater displacement

See Camera Settings for details about each setting.
