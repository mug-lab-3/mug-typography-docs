Quick Start ​

Steps to introduce Mug Typography and display your first text.

Installation ​

  1. Place the downloaded plugin files into DaVinci Resolve's OFX plugin folder.
  2. Restart DaVinci Resolve.

Basic Usage ​

  1. Drag and drop Mug Typography from the Effects Library onto your timeline.
  2. Open the Inspector and type your text.
