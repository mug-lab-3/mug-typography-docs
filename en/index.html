TEXT DISSECTION

Break characters down to their component parts

Goes beyond splitting text into characters: each character is automatically dissected into the "parts" it is built from, following the font data. Every part exposes its own detailed parameters, enabling expressions of unprecedented precision.

INTERACTIVE CONTROLS

Direct manipulation in the preview window

Drag text position, pivot, rotation, scale and more right in the DaVinci Resolve preview window. Refine your design intuitively, without hunting through inspector parameters.

AUTO ALIGNMENT

Natural re-layout, fully automatic

As characters scale and rotate, their positions are recalculated and realigned automatically. No more nudging characters into place one by one.

COPY & PASTE

Duplicate styles in an instant

Copy stroke widths, fill colors, animation keyframes and other parameters from one character or part, and paste them onto another with a single click. A massive boost for typography work built on repeated styles.

GRADIENT

Smooth colors built on human perception

Colors are interpolated in perceptually uniform color spaces, producing smooth, vivid gradients free of muddy midtones. Beautiful color transitions with no tedious tweaking.

WRITE ON

Draw text in any order

Reveal text in whatever order you like — down to individual parts. Handwriting-style write-on animations take nothing more than a couple of keyframes.

DEFORM

Built-in animations that just work

A collection of unique animations that deform the character outlines themselves. Tick a checkbox and they come alive — with parameters to fine-tune every detail.
