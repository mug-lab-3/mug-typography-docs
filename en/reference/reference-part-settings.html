PARAMETER REFERENCE

Part Settings

Transform and color the dots and strokes that make up a character on a per-part basis. This is the most granular editing level available in standard presets.

↗TransformPosition, pivot, and rotation↘Z3D DepthPer-part depth↘◐ColorPer-part fill color↘

This page configures individual transformations for parts selected in Part selection mode.

IMPORTANT

Limitations in Lite presets
Individual part settings are completely disabled in the lightweight Lite presets (Lite Low / Lite Mid / Lite High / Lite 3D). The Part Settings page in the Inspector is hidden, and parts cannot be selected or edited individually in the viewer (Part selection mode). Use a standard preset (Low / Mid / High / 3D) if you need to adjust individual parts. The number of parameter slots varies depending on the selected preset (Low / Mid / High) and the settings in preferences.json. See “Limitations” in the appendix for details.

Transformations are applied in the following order: Global Transform → Character Transform → Part Transform.

ParameterDescriptionDefault

Transforms in 3D Presets NEW ​

Stretch is not displayed and cannot be used in standard 3D presets.

Z Offset is added to adjust the depth of a part. Position, Pivot, Scale, and Rotation remain available.
